Crypto Tracker

Founded Fall 2020 by Ahmed, Noah and Omar, Crypto Tracker provides a view of top cryptocurrencies, ability to search detailed information about any active cryptocurrency, as well as display recent headlines regarding the world of Blockchain and crytpocurrencies. Crypto Tracker is a Dashboard that tracks cryptoassets in the rapidly growing cryptocurrency space. We mainly focus on the top 25 cryptocurrency that is leading the market at any given time.
